Who am I?

I am a Games Design student studying at The University for the Creative Arts, I'm currently in my second year at University.

I've been making games off and on for about four years now, but I've only just started making good games at Uni. I try to keep up my skills in 3D moddeling as much as possible and try to keep up with new software that intrigues me.

Outside of work I love to cook, which usually ends up being whatever overly complex recipe I see online and I've got a love for reading, particularly pulpy fantasy novels along with occasionally playing the drums.


I'm not normally made out text but for the sake of this websites aestetics I am. Don't worry if you zoom out I look more personable.
