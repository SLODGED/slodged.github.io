FOOLS GOLD

After a long year of development, my final major project, FOOLS GOLD, is complete! It is an ambitious Action Role-Playing Game where you wake to find yourself imprisoned in a medieval mining colony far away from the heart of the Kingdom. FOOLS GOLD features a complete character creation along with blood pumping combat.

Wait here for progress updates and behind the scenes to come!
