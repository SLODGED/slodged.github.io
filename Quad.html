Section Summary:

Q.U.A.D. was the first project in my Rapid Games Prototyping unit, the prompt for this was "30 Second minigame". I quickly started coming up with ideas and landed on a drone racing game quite quickly.

I am quite proud of this game but I wished I had spent more time on making the level look pretty.


Oh and Q.U.A.D. doesn't actually stand for anything, in development I called it "QUAD WING" and "DRONE ZERO" and eventually came up with better name.


This game features fairly basic local coop, and was very fun to play with other people.

The majority of the movement is the stock movement component that comes with Unreal Engine's Third Person Character. I added in controls to make it gain and lose altitude along with rotation to the left/right movement.

The main issue I encountered was after the actor rotated itself, how would I return its rotation back to its original position.

Eventually I decided to have a custom event trigger every 0.0025 seconds to try and reset the roll of the actor as seen here:


This is the Drone, like all of the games made in my Rapid Games Prototyping Unit I modelled them in Maya, anitmated them in Blender, and textured them in Substance painter.

As a running theme in these projects the main player model was the first thing made and this drone is no exception.
