CRUD KINGDOM

Curd Kingdom is a boomer shooter where you play as a block of Swiss cheese. I’ve had this idea for ages and been meaning to do something with it. This prototype was my first real use of Actor Components, something I wish I had used earlier.

Overall, very fun to make but I ended up abandoning it as I quickly ran out of cheese-based puns.

Here are some fun enemy names I came up with:

  • Cheese Child
  • Brie Boy
  • Gruyere Girl
  • Emmental Exterminator
  • Parm Police
  • Swiss Solider
