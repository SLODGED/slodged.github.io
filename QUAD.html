Q.U.A.D.

Q.U.A.D. was the first game made for my Rapid Games Prototyping unit. The brief was to create a 30 second mini game, I came up with the idea of Drone Racing immediately and started working on a prototype. I really struggled with creating the movement, but I learnt a lot from it. The final game is a Quad Copter racing game where you have 30 seconds to complete the track.

The prototype was made in three weeks; I rushed the final level because I wanted to start work on the next game in the unit which would become FLESH GUN: ADRENALINE JUNKIE.

I've been meaing to do a mobile port for the longest time, maybe one day I'll get around to it.
